## General Template Customization Guide

### How to Use These Templates

1. **Customize for Your Context**: Update links, names, and specific requirements
2. **Set Clear Expectations**: Review checklist with new team member on day one
3. **Regular Check-ins**: Schedule weekly check-ins first month, then bi-weekly
4. **Track Progress**: Use checklist to track completion and identify blockers
5. **Gather Feedback**: Ask for feedback at 2 weeks and 1 month
6. **Iterate**: Update templates based on feedback and lessons learned

### Adding Role-Specific Items

When customizing templates, consider:
- **Company-specific processes**: Add internal procedures and policies
- **Tool-specific training**: Include training for your specific tech stack
- **Team norms**: Add team-specific working agreements
- **Domain knowledge**: Include industry or domain-specific learning
- **Compliance**: Add any regulatory or compliance training

### Buddy/Mentor Support

Schedule regular sync-ups:
- **Week 1**: Daily 15-minute check-ins
- **Week 2**: Every other day check-ins
- **Week 3-4**: Twice weekly check-ins
- **Month 2+**: Weekly check-ins

### Manager Support

Formal check-ins:
- **End of Week 1**: Initial settling-in feedback
- **End of Week 2**: Process understanding and tool comfort
- **End of Week 4**: Comprehensive 30-day review
- **End of Month 3**: 90-day performance discussion
