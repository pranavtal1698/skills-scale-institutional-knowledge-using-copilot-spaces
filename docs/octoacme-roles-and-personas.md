# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing specialists validate product quality, ensure acceptance criteria are met, and identify defects before release. They collaborate with developers and product teams to deliver reliable software.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Report and track defects
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Participate in release readiness reviews

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch issues early in the development cycle
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Bug triage meetings
- Test status reports and release sign-offs

### Interactions
- Works with Developers (bug reports, test requirements)
- Collaborates with Product Manager (acceptance criteria validation)
- Coordinates with DevOps Engineer (test automation in CI/CD)

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Leads the creation of intuitive, accessible interfaces; ensures product design aligns with user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Develop wireframes, flows, and prototypes
- Facilitate design reviews
- Collaborate with Product Manager, Project Manager, and Developers
- Maintain the design system
- Conduct user research and usability testing

### Goals
- Deliver consistent, user-friendly design
- Shorten design-to-development cycle
- Advocate for accessibility best practices
- Ensure design aligns with brand and user expectations

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design syncs
- Participation in sprint planning and demos
- Design review sessions
- User research presentations

### Interactions
- Works with Product Manager (requirements, priorities, user needs)
- Collaborates with Developers (handoff, feasibility, implementation)
- Coordinates with Business Analyst (user workflows and requirements)

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Ensures robust, reliable build/deployment processes and infrastructure. Bridges development and operations to enable continuous delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Configure and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Automate testing and deployments
- Monitor, alert, and respond to incidents
- Manage infrastructure as code
- Ensure system reliability and performance

### Goals
- Improve deployment speed and reliability
- Minimize downtime and operational risk
- Enable developer productivity through automation
- Maintain system observability

### Typical Communication
- Deployment status reports
- Incident notifications
- Infrastructure planning meetings
- Post-mortem reviews

### Interactions
- Works with Developers (deployment/process alignment, troubleshooting)
- Collaborates with QA (test and release automation)
- Coordinates with Project Manager (release schedules)
- Partners with Security Lead (secure pipelines and infrastructure)

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Translates business needs into actionable requirements; clarifies objectives and measures of success.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document requirements
- Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Support user story creation and refinement
- Create process documentation and workflows
- Validate solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure clear, validated requirements
- Improve post-release adoption/metrics
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements

### Typical Communication
- Requirement workshops
- Story refinement meetings
- Stakeholder interviews
- Business review presentations

### Interactions
- Works closely with Project Manager and Product Manager (requirements definition)
- Engages with Stakeholders (business objectives, process analysis)
- Collaborates with Developers (clarifying acceptance criteria)
- Partners with UX/UI Designer (user workflows and experience)

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Acts as main point of contact for customer feedback/incidents post-release. Ensures support readiness and channels user insights back to the team.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and escalate user issues
- Ensure support readiness prior to launches
- Provide feedback on recurring user pain points
- Maintain support documentation and knowledge base
- Train support team on new features

### Goals
- Rapid resolution of user incidents
- Improve product through support insights
- Minimize customer impact during issues
- Enable self-service support

### Typical Communication
- Post-release reviews
- Incident channels and escalations
- Weekly feedback summaries
- Launch readiness meetings

### Interactions
- Works with Project Manager/Developers on prioritizing fixes
- Shares insights with Product Manager (feature improvements, pain points)
- Coordinates with QA (reproducing and validating issues)
- Partners with DevOps Engineer (incident response)

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Oversees security requirements, reviews architectures, manages security incidents. Champions security best practices across the organization.

### Responsibilities
- Define and monitor security controls
- Lead response to security issues
- Promote best practices across team
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Manage vulnerability remediation
- Ensure compliance with security standards

### Goals
- Reduce vulnerabilities and security risks
- Increase team security awareness
- Maintain secure development practices
- Respond effectively to security incidents

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews and assessments
- Regular security briefings
- Incident response coordination
- Vulnerability status updates

### Interactions
- Works with Developers/DevOps (secure pipelines/code, security tooling)
- Collaborates with Project Manager and stakeholders (risk updates, timelines)
- Partners with Business Analyst (security requirements)
- Coordinates with Support Lead (security incident response)
